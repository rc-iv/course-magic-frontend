Algebra 2: Course Syllabus

Course Title

Algebra 2

Course Description

This course aims to deepen student's; understanding of algebra by focusing on polynomial expressions, complex numbers, and logarithmic functions. Students will gain a strong foundation in algebraic concepts that will prepare them for higher-level math courses.

By the end of this course, students should be able to:
